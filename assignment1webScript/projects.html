My Projects

Choice-O-Mat

Choice-O-Mat is a website designed to make it easier to choose between two degree programmes. It is an interactive questionnaire in which the questions are asked and answered in a playful way. At the end, you are given the degree programme that suits you best.

Gemeinsam Gut

An app that brings both parties, young and old together to make everyday life easier for the older generation, enhancing the joy of life. It's about community and cohesion among each other.

Infinite Dreams

In this visually striking silent film, the story unfolds through the lens of a young individual navigating the complexities of living alone. Set against the backdrop of an ordinary day, the film explores the delicate balance between moments of joy and deep melancholy, all without the use of dialogue.
